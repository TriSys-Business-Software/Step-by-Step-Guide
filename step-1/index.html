Step 1: Getting Started


This is the first stage in the step by step guide to building a recruitment agency web site which uses the TriSys Web API to search, display and update recruiting data held in TriSys' cloud. The following steps will be conducted in sequence, although some steps may be skipped depending upon the business requirements of the recruitment agency.

Index / Steps

1. Procure an operational, fully editable website

The main prerequisite to building an interactive jobs portal for a recruitment agency is to start with a hosted web site to which a web designer has full access. This web site can be sourced from one of the many web site template services and typically cost under $50. once the web site is operational and has the branding of the recruitment agency, then it can be edited by copying and pasting html and javascript from this step-by-step guide to build a compelling and interactive candidate oriented recruitment agency web site with live jobs from the agency CRM database.

2. Searching & displaying list of vacancies Step 2

Candidates visiting the recruitment agency web site will see a list of live vacancies. Candidates can refine their search using job title, start date, location, salary/rate or any other type of search they require. Candidates can drill into one or more vacancies and add to their favourites or apply.

3. Viewing individual vacancy summary Step 3

After searching for vacancies and drilling into one or more vacancies, candidates can add the selected vacancy to their favourites or apply.

4. Contact us Step 4

Build a contact-us page to capture details of a candidate (or client) who wishes to engage with the recruitment agency. The details are logged into the agency CRM system and automated template driven e-mail messages are sent to the requester and agency consultants.

5. Candidate registration Step 5

Candidates can register by filling in a form. The details are logged into the agency CRM system and automated template driven e-mail messages are sent to the candidate and agency consultants.

6. Login Step 6

Candidates who have registered, or have been sent their login credentials by the recruitment agency, can login to the recruitment agency web site and apply to vacancies, or maintain their candidate profile such as updating their availability. work preferences or uploading a new CV.

7. Forgotten password Step 7

Candidates who have previously registered or are held in the recruitment agency CRM system, may request their password, or request a password reset using the form built in this step.

8. Logoff Step 8

Candidates who are logged in can logout using this step.

9. Update candidate profile Step 9

Logged in candidates can maintain their candidate profile such as updating their availability. work preferences or uploading a new CV.

10. Favourite vacancies Step 10

Logged in candidates can add selected vacancies to a list of favourites from where they can apply to one or more in a single operation.

11. Applying to vacancies Step 11

Candidates who are logged in can apply to one or more vacancies and automatically alert the managing recruitment consultant together with sending automated template driven e-mail messages to the candidate confirming their application. Candidate details are logged against the shortlist of applied vacancies allowing recruitment consultants to quickly and efficiently progress each application using the underlying CRM system.

12. Manage vacancy applications Step 12

Candidates can apply to many vacancies so this helps them keep track of which vacancies they are shortlisted for including their interview schedule and any feedback from recruiters or clients.
